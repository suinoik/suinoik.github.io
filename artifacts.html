A reflective artifact documenting my growth as an emerging leader through self-assessment, with a focus on AI integration, adaptability, and change management.

Objective

To reflect on my experience completing the AI/ML Integration Leadership Skills Self-Assessment and the Change Management Skills Self-Assessment, and to examine how these tools contributed to my professional growth as an emerging leader.

Process

I completed both self-assessments to evaluate my current readiness to lead in areas related to artificial intelligence, machine learning integration, and organizational change. Tools

AI/ML Integration Leadership Skills Self-Assessment, Change Management Skills Self-Assessment, reflective writing, and portfolio development tools.

Value Proposition

This artifact demonstrates my ability to engage in meaningful self-assessment, reflect critically on my leadership development, and position myself as a learner who leads with curiosity, growth, and intentionality.

This artifact documents my experience completing the AI/ML Integration Leadership Skills Self-Assessment and the Change Management Skills Self-Assessment. While these assessments may appear to be simple evaluation tools, they represent an important first step in professional leadership development. Many professionals attempt to lead technological or organizational change without first evaluating their own readiness, knowledge gaps, and leadership approach.

Moving forward, the insights gained from these assessments will guide my professional development. I plan to continue expanding my knowledge of AI and machine learning systems, strengthening my change leadership skills, and applying reflective practices to evaluate my progress.

Artifact 03

AInsight Newsletter – Commercial AI Applications Across Industries

A collaborative newsletter project analyzing how commercial AI applications are transforming healthcare, semiconductors, and legal services.

Objective

To research and present new or updated commercial applications of artificial intelligence across three different industries in a newsletter format that is engaging, accessible, and professionally structured.

Process

This project was completed collaboratively with a team. Together, we selected healthcare, semiconductors, and legal services because they aligned closely with our academic and professional interests. Within that effort, I contributed to researching commercial AI applications, analyzing their impact on business processes, and developing content for the newsletter.

Tools

Industry research sources, AI-assisted content development, collaborative planning tools, and newsletter design tools.

Value Proposition

This artifact demonstrates my ability to collaborate effectively within a team while contributing meaningful individual analysis. It highlights my skills in researching emerging AI applications, evaluating their business impact across multiple industries, and communicating technical developments in a clear and accessible format.

This artifact is a newsletter titled The AInsight, created to examine how artificial intelligence is actively reshaping healthcare, semiconductors, and law. Rather than discussing AI in general terms, this project focuses on practical commercial applications already influencing how organizations operate.

In the healthcare section, we explored how Abridge and OpenEvidence are helping reduce administrative burden and support clinical decision-making. In the semiconductor section, we focused on NVIDIA’s use of AI in chip design and manufacturing, showing how machine learning is accelerating workflows such as lithography, routing, and timing analysis. In the legal section, we examined Thomson Reuters CoCounsel as an example of AI being used to automate document review, legal research, and workflow support in high-stakes professional environments.

One of the most important insights from this project is that AI becomes most valuable when it is embedded directly into existing professional workflows. Across all three industries, the pattern is not simply automation for its own sake, but strategic augmentation that improves efficiency and decision quality.

Working as part of a team also strengthened my ability to collaborate on research-driven projects. It required aligning ideas, dividing responsibilities effectively, and ensuring that our final output maintained a consistent voice and structure.

Overall, this artifact represents my ability to connect AI innovation with real business transformation while working collaboratively. It demonstrates research, critical analysis, teamwork, and professional communication skills.
